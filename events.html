Past Events

Here are the events/meetups that we organized in the past


Meetup #3: (CloudNative - joint meetup Google Cloud Community + Container Devs)

Glad to announce another edition of Cloud Native event on 8th September which will be a joint meetup with Google Cloud Developer Community, Bangalore and Container Developers. First session would be from IBM where we would get to hear insights from a container expert on upcoming workload isolation and encryption technologies in container ecosystem


Meetup #2: Google Cloud Next’18 Extended : Bangalore

Google Cloud Next’18 is the annual developer festival at Google, and we hosted one here in Bangalore at Google office as an Extended event! Hear from Google Cloud leaders about how the cloud is transforming business, making Artificial Intelligence available to everyone, and improving the lives and circumstances of people around the world in ways never before possible.


Meetup #1: Joint Meetup (with Cloud Native + Container Devs) - Containers/K8S/Serverless

This is a joint meetup with "Cloud Native" and "Container Developers" group. The meetup is focused on Server-less (Cloud Functions), So we will be having a talk on Server-less/Cloud Functions followed by Hands-On-Lab. Target Audience are "Beginners", so basic understanding of Containers are the only prerequisite. We also have a special talk from "Hasura" about their "GraphQL Engine".
